PRIVACY POLICY

Effective Date: March 31, 2026

1. DATA COLLECTION

  • We collect personal identification information including name, email address, and phone number when voluntarily submitted via our website contact forms.
  • Browser-specific data, including IP addresses and access times, is collected automatically for security and analytical purposes.
  • Payment details for pre-orders are processed via encrypted third-party gateways; Yummy Kurtos does not store full credit card numbers on its local servers.

2. PURPOSE OF PROCESSING

  • To facilitate the preparation and collection of chimney cake orders at our Brighton shop or market stall.
  • To fulfill legal obligations regarding food safety and transaction record-keeping.
  • To provide localized updates regarding our trading schedule within the Sussex area.

3. DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY

  • Data is stored in secure, password-protected environments with access restricted to authorized personnel only.
  • Personal information is retained only for the duration required by UK tax and consumer law (typically 6 years).
  • We implement SSL (Secure Sockets Layer) technology to protect data transmission across the site.
