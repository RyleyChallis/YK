COOKIE POLICY

Status: Compliant with UK GDPR

1. DEFINITION OF COOKIES

  • Cookies are small text files stored on your computer or mobile device when you visit our website.
  • They allow the site to remember your actions and preferences over a period of time.
  • We use both "session" cookies (which expire when you close your browser) and "persistent" cookies (which stay until you delete them).

2. UTILIZATION

  • Strictly Necessary: Required for the basic operation of the site, such as navigating between sections.
  • Performance: Used to track how visitors move through the site so we can optimize the "Visit Us" and "Menu" pages.
  • Functionality: Remembers choices you make, such as localized settings for the Brighton area.

3. CONTROL MECHANISMS

  • Users may block or delete cookies via their browser settings at any time.
  • Blocking all cookies may result in certain parts of the Yummy Kurtos website failing to load correctly.
  • Third-party links (like Google Maps) may set their own cookies outside of our direct control.
